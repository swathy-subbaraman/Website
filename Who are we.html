What we focus on?

Being Human is the primary slogan of this society. Without focusing on caste, race, or language, support has been extended
to the struggling lives in the society. We,The Aashraya Charitable Trust, take an oath to serve and support every life 
with all the might we have.

How is your fund used?

The amount that is donated, is distributed to various orphanages that are under the wing of Aashraya Charitable Trust, who in turn,
use it for the food,cloth and educational expenses of the young minds and other medical expenses of senior citizens. This money is 
also spent for health camps,blood donation camps and various other awareness camps. People below the poverty line, who seek medical help
are funded with the donations collected, thus saving many lives. Apart from collecting money, we collect food, used clothes and books,
which are sent to many orphanages to feed, clothe and educate the children there. This facility is open only to major cities and we are
trying to bring it up to other cities in the nation.
