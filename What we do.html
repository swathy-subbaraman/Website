We, The Aashraya Charitable Society, spend 90% of our donations in serving the needy, in various ways, mainly focusing on the poorer strata if the society. To sum up Our works so far, include, adoption of 10 schools in the rural area of Tamilnadu, and distribution of books to special schools (Schools for deaf & dumb, Schools for special children, Schools for Autistic children). We have organised many blood donation camps in association with 'The Lions Club'.Various competitions have been organised on special occasions like Independence Day,Gandhi Jayanti,Republic Day and exciting prizes have been given.





Our organization always gives prime importance to the child rights which are violated to many children now a days,especially to those children who are poor and orphan. We put all efforts to make sure that every child in their countries can enjoy all the rights in this Convention. Every child has the right to be alive. We make sure that children survive and develop in the best possible way. We make sure that all children gets basic rights such as education,shelter, clothes,food. Besides,we also make children of Aashraya to get involved in various extra curricular activities such as drawing classes,music classes,dance classes etc. Many people across India volunteer to take classes freely. Our children are also active in sports. Our students actively participate in many competitions and have won many prizes.


The volunteers of Aashraya organization conduct many awareness campaigns every year in order to make people aware about the organization and the works we do.Many people across India get involved in this organisation after attending our awareness campaigns and donate to our team.As we conducted many awareness campaigns in different places,many people came forward to help and took interest to be the member of our organization.We have done many one day as weel as three to four days campaings where we gave awareness in places like colleges and schools. Students have given many useful things like books,hygenic clothes etc. which benifited our organisation. Many young students who are involved in many welfare clubs in colleges and schools such as Youth Red Cross(YRC), National Service Scheme(NSS) etc. has visited and has interacted with the people of our organization.




Health is not something we can buy, but many good hearts are trying to get out of some serious health issues. We try to share their struggle. We actively help to those who come requesting for treatment payments. The donations that are collected, Sponsors from various corners of the World, and many notable celebrities help us to make a change in many people's lives.


Education is a powerful driver of development and one of the strongest instruments for reducing poverty and improving health, gender equality & peace. We, The Aashraya Charitable Society, believe that education is one of the crucial factor which can change the fate of a nation. Thus, we put our best step forward in educating young minds across the country, with the books that are received as donations and new books are bought every year. The Aashraya Charitable Society has adopted 10 schools in the State of Tamilnadu, in the districts of Kanchipuram, Madurai, Kanniyakumari and Nilgiris. We are proud that these schools which were in a pathetic condition, were renovated, chairs and benches that were in cracks were all replaced, and new amenities like Laboratories, were all done exclusively by The Aashraya Charitable Society. The above mentioned schools were adopted in the year of 2020 and are still under our maintenance. We are expanding our territory and serving many bright students and are looking forward to adopt more schools in the future.
